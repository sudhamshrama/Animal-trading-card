Beagle dog

The beagle is a scent hound,developed primarily for hunting hare.

  • Color: White,Chocolate,Brown.
  • Features: Determined,Excitable.
  • Height: 38cm.
  • Weight: 10kg.
  • Life expectancy: 15 years.

The beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar to appearance to the much larger foxhound.
